About the Bus Management System

Our Bus Management System is designed to make commuting easy and efficient. Users can book their bus seats online, view real-time bus tracking, and check schedules to avoid unnecessary wait times.

This project is developed by students of Model Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET), Jammu, as a comprehensive academic exercise.
